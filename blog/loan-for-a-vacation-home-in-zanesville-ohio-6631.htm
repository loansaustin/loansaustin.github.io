Loan for a Vacation Home in Zanesville, Ohio

Home / Blog / Blog Details

Why Zanesville, Ohio, Is the Perfect Spot for Your Vacation Home

Nestled in the heart of the Midwest, Zanesville, Ohio, offers a unique blend of small-town charm and outdoor adventure, making it an ideal location for a vacation home. With its scenic rivers, historic landmarks, and affordable real estate, Zanesville is quickly becoming a hotspot for second-home buyers. Whether you’re looking for a peaceful retreat or a base for exploring Ohio’s natural beauty, this city has something for everyone.

The Appeal of Zanesville’s Real Estate Market

Unlike overcrowded coastal destinations, Zanesville provides a more relaxed and budget-friendly alternative. The median home price here is significantly lower than in popular vacation areas, meaning your dollar stretches further. Plus, with rising interest in rural and semi-rural living post-pandemic, investing in a vacation home here could be a wise financial move.

How to Secure a Loan for Your Zanesville Vacation Home

Financing a second home isn’t the same as buying a primary residence. Lenders often have stricter requirements, but with the right approach, you can secure a competitive loan.

Understanding Loan Options

  1. Conventional Mortgage Loans – These are the most common for vacation homes, typically requiring a higher down payment (often 20-30%) and a strong credit score (720+).
  2. Home Equity Loans (HELOCs) – If you already own a primary residence, tapping into your home’s equity can be a cost-effective way to fund your vacation property.
  3. Portfolio Loans – Some local banks and credit unions offer flexible terms for second homes, especially if you’re investing in a growing market like Zanesville.

Tips for Getting Approved

  • Boost Your Credit Score – Pay down debts and avoid new credit applications before applying.
  • Save for a Larger Down Payment – A higher down payment reduces risk for lenders, potentially lowering your interest rate.
  • Shop Around for Lenders – Local Ohio lenders may offer better terms than national banks.

The Financial Benefits of Owning a Vacation Home in Zanesville

Beyond personal enjoyment, a vacation home can be a smart investment. Here’s why:

Rental Income Potential

With platforms like Airbnb and Vrbo, renting out your property when you’re not using it can help offset mortgage costs. Zanesville’s growing tourism—thanks to attractions like the Wilds and Dillon State Park—means steady rental demand.

Tax Advantages

  • Mortgage Interest Deductions – You may deduct interest on loans up to $750,000 (for primary and secondary homes combined).
  • Property Tax Deductions – Ohio’s property taxes are relatively low, but they’re still deductible.
  • Depreciation for Rental Use – If you rent the home part-time, you could qualify for additional tax benefits.

Addressing Today’s Economic Challenges

Rising Interest Rates

The Federal Reserve’s rate hikes have made borrowing more expensive. However, locking in a fixed-rate mortgage now could save you money if rates climb higher.

Inflation and Housing Supply

While inflation has driven up costs, Zanesville’s market remains more stable than overheated urban areas. Limited housing supply in vacation destinations means your property could appreciate over time.

Climate Change Considerations

Unlike coastal properties vulnerable to hurricanes or wildfires, Zanesville’s inland location offers long-term stability. Investing here avoids the risks associated with climate-prone regions.

Making Your Dream Vacation Home a Reality

If Zanesville sounds like the perfect getaway, start by:
- Researching neighborhoods (e.g., near Muskingum River for waterfront properties).
- Getting pre-approved to strengthen your offer in a competitive market.
- Working with a local realtor who understands vacation home trends.

With careful planning, your Zanesville vacation home could be both a personal sanctuary and a savvy financial move in today’s unpredictable economy.
